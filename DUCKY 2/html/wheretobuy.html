Ducks In The Window

Ducks In The Window® (DITW) started as a unique concept store located in the heart of Chatham, Cape Cod that quickly evolved into the worlds largest retail rubber duck shop. Today we manage both retail and wholesale customers with all their rubber duck product needs including brand logos, designing, and manufacturing unique one of a kind creations.

Ducky City

“A perfect rubber ducky for every occasion!” At Ducky City, we believe the timeless simplicity of rubber ducks will always be a sound of joy for any household. THat’s why we’re always dedicating time to create rubber ducks that will go great with any special occasion or fit in perfectly with your home.

Amsterdam Duck Store

Welcome to our online duck store and meet the cutest rubber ducks of Amsterdam. They’re all premium ducks made of high quality materials and CE approved. Discover the hand painted details and special finishing. Absolute collectors items. Take your pick. Order online worldwide or visit our duck stores in Amsterdam, Barcelona, Sevilla, San Sebastian, Lisbon, Florence, Limassol, San Marino, Madeira, Milan, Rome and Paris. Have a nice duck!
