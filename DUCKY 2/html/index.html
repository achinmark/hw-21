What is Rubber Ducky Collecting?



A hobby. A passion. A lifestyle.


Whether you’re a newbie or a pro, let DUCKDUCKWHO.COM help you hunt for a new favorite toy to add to your collection.

Rubber toys first appeared in the late 1800s, when manufacturers made use of Charles Goodyear’s process for rendering rubber into malleable material. By the 1940s, rubber ducks developed into the iconic floating yellow figure with bright orange bill we recognize today. Collectors proudly display variations of the classic form that proclaim their affinity to colleges, careers, sports, celebrities, and holidays. (https://www.toyhalloffame.org/toys/rubber-duck)
