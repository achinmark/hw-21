Get Started and Get Collecting


Method A: Purchase Online

Step 1: Buy Online

Open a web browser, such as Google, on a mobile or desktop device. Click on "Shopping" and search for "rubber duck" to get started. You can add different descriptors to find different ducks.

Step 2: Wait for Delivery

Depending on which website you purchase from, your rubber duckies may be shipped overnight or even in a couple months (especially if you snag a rare one from overseas). Be patient and continue to track your package until your duckies arrive safely to your home.

Step 3: Play time!

If your rubber duckies are here, it's time to celebrate! You can either play with them in the bathtub, display them on a shelf and show them off, package them away for safekeeping, or even trade with another collector! If you take a picture and share on social media, be sure to tag us (@duckduckwho) in your post.


Method B: Purchase In-Store

Step 1: Buy In-Store

Buy in-store. If you don't know of any local toy stores near by, you'll want to search for directions on a mobile or desktop device. If you are searching for a particular ducky, the store employees can help you find or order one using their catalog.

Step 2: Check-Out

The perk of buying your rubber duckies in-store is that you'll receive the product immediately and won't have to pay or wait for shipping (unless it's a special or back order). It's great to support local stores as well!

Step 3: Play time!

If your rubber duckies are here, it's time to celebrate! You can either play with them in the bathtub, display them on a shelf and show them off, package them away for safekeeping, or even trade with another collector! If you take a picture and share on social media, be sure to tag us (@duckduckwho) in your post.
